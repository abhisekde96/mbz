About Us

Trusted Partner in
Global Health Infrastructure

MBZ Group is an ISO 9001:2015–certified company established in 2013 and based in Dubai, UAE. We specialize in international sourcing, supply, and logistics for United Nations agencies, NGOs, and government organizations worldwide.

Operating in full compliance with international trade regulations, we apply strict quality control standards, transparent processes, and a strong commitment to on-time delivery. Our highly experienced team delivers reliable, cost-effective, and compliant trading solutions.
